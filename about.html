Imagining and creating products that enrich people's lives and make a lasting positive impact is what really inspires me.

From a young age, I was fascinated by the world of digital experiences and visual design. This journey began when PlayStation One entered my life and introduced me to the immersive world of 3D graphics and electronic music through a game called Wipeout (1996).

Countless hours of gameplay led me to ask: "How is something so immersive created?" The answer came when I discovered that the game's user interface and visual identity were created by The Designers Republic, a small studio in Sheffield, England.
